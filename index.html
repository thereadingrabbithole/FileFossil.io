🦴 FileFossil.io

Recover. Download. Preserve.

Let’s face it – the internet is a hot mess. Pages vanish, content gets nuked, and your favourite memes disappear like your willpower during Dry January. That’s where we come in. FileFossil.io helps you recover lost websites, scrape media files, and hoard the good stuff before it’s gone.

  • 🕸️ Archive.org/Wayback website recovery
  • 🖼️ Bulk image and video scraping
  • 📁 File extraction (PDFs, docs, zips... if it’s downloadable, it’s fossilised)

Think of us as the Indiana Jones of dead websites. Minus the whip. For now.
